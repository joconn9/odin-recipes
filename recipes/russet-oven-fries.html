Russet Oven Fries

Overview

4 servings - 48 cal/serving

Approx 25 minutes

French Fries! Yum! I love french fries, but I am also trying to watch what I eat. These fries allow me to indulge in one of my favorite foods, without all the fat! Win/win! Pro tip: after the fries are done cooking, throw them under the broiler for some extra crunch!

Ingredients

  • 2 large russet potatoes, sliced into 1/4" thick lengthwise slices
  • 1 egg white, lighly beaten
  • 1/4 tsp paprika
  • salt and pepper (to taste)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Lightly coat baking sheet with cooking spray.
  3. Brush the potatoes with the egg white and sprinkle with paprika.
  4. Bake for approximately 20 minutes, or until browned.
  5. Salt and pepper to taste.
